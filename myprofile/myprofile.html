Decription

My Profile - Manage your profile information.

My Profile helps you to manage your profile information You can store your profile information and use it later. You can also share your information with others.

All information you saved with My Profile stays on your phone. Any information doesn't leave your phone. My Profile requires no special permissions to run. My Profile is an offline app. It means it doesn't need an internet connection to run. So, all your information is secure.

Inforamations you can store with My Profile are:
  • Cover Photos
  • Profile Photos
  • User Name
  • User Bio
  • Contact Details
    • - Phone
    • - Email
    • - Username
    • - URL
    • - Other
  • Address Informations
    • - Address Name
    • - Address Details
  • Work Informations
    • - Institution Name
    • - Position Name
    • - Working From
    • - Working To
  • Education Informations
    • - Institution Name
    • - Subject Name
    • - From
    • - To
You will get usage info of My Profile:
  • Last Data Change
  • Last Backup Created

To make you know when you have updated your information in My Profile. And The Last Backup you have created.

Other features available in My Profile are:
  • Dark Mode
  • Backup
  • Restore
